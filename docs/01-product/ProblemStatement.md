## Purpose

This document describes the business problem Merchant Harmony is intended to solve.

---

## Problem

Many small and medium-sized businesses have limited ways to collect, organize, and respond to customer feedback.

Feedback is often scattered across paper forms, social media, review sites, or messaging platforms, making it difficult to engage customers, identify recurring issues, and improve services.

Businesses also lack a simple mechanism to build direct relationships with customers after a transaction.

---

## Proposed Solution

Merchant Harmony provides a lightweight platform where:

- Customers can easily connect with a merchant by scanning a QR Code.
- Customers can submit feedback through structured conversations.
- Merchants can respond directly within the same feedback thread.
- Feedback remains organized and searchable for future improvements.

The platform is intentionally designed to be extensible so that future capabilities can be added without redesigning the core system.

---

## Initial Product Focus

The first release focuses on validating the core interaction between a merchant and a customer.

The MVP intentionally excludes advanced capabilities such as loyalty programs, promotions, notifications, analytics, and AI-assisted insights until the core workflow is proven.

---

## Success Indicators

The initial product is successful when:

- Customer onboarding is simple.
- Merchant onboarding is simple.
- Customer feedback is easy to submit.
- Merchant responses are easy to manage.
- The platform provides a clean foundation for future expansion.
