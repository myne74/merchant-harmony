## Purpose

This document defines the long-term vision for Merchant Harmony and serves as the guiding direction for product and engineering decisions.

---

## Vision Statement

Merchant Harmony aims to become a simple, extensible platform that enables merchants to build stronger relationships with their customers through meaningful engagement and actionable feedback.

The platform starts with customer feedback and merchant interaction, while providing a foundation for future capabilities such as customer engagement, promotions, loyalty, analytics, and AI-assisted business insights.

---

## Product Philosophy

The project follows a "build small, build well" approach.

Rather than implementing every possible feature upfront, Merchant Harmony focuses on delivering a clean, production-quality foundation that can evolve incrementally without major redesign.

Every new capability should build upon the existing architecture while preserving simplicity, maintainability, and scalability.

---

## Engineering Vision

Merchant Harmony is more than an application—it is a demonstration of a professional software engineering lifecycle.

The repository is intended to showcase:

- Product thinking
- Requirements engineering
- Solution architecture
- API-first design
- Secure backend development
- Clean software design
- Operational readiness
- Continuous evolution

---

## Success Criteria

The vision is considered successful when:

- Merchants can easily engage with customers.
- The platform remains easy to extend with new capabilities.
- The codebase reflects production-quality engineering practices.
- Documentation and implementation remain aligned throughout the project's lifecycle.
